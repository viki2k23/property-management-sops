# Tenant Maintenance Request SOP

## Objective

Handle tenant maintenance requests quickly, professionally, and with proper documentation.

## Process

1. Receive tenant complaint by call, text, email, or portal.
2. Confirm issue details.
3. Request photos or video if needed.
4. Identify urgency level.
5. Assign technician or vendor.
6. Confirm appointment time with tenant.
7. Follow up after repair completion.
8. Save invoice, photos, and notes.

## Priority Levels

### Emergency
- Water leak
- No heat in winter
- Electrical hazard
- Gas smell

### Urgent
- Toilet blockage
- Appliance failure
- Lock issue

### Standard
- Cosmetic repairs
- Minor adjustments
- Non-urgent maintenance

## Records Required

- Date reported
- Tenant name
- Address
- Vendor assigned
- Cost
- Completion date
